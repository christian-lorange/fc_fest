FoCoMX 2019

The Fort Collins Musicians Association (FoCoMA) is proud to present the 11th Annual Fort Collins Music eXperiment (FoCoMX) on April 26 & 27, 2019.

We are so lucky to have such a vibrant scene that is supported by music lovers and our community at large. Thank you to everyone for making such an event possible! FoCoMX, presented by the nonprofit Fort Collins Musicians Association (FoCoMA) is 2-day festival with well over 200 artists. Afternoon and evening performances offer a wealth of family-friendly programming. Later-evening performances feature rock, singer-songwriter, bluegrass, funk, reggae and more.

FoCoMX started in 2009 as a grassroots experiment in promoting local music. The festival has grown over the years and we now see attendance of approximately 5500 and has garnering national attention and earning FoCoMX the unofficial title of “The Biggest Little Festivalin America.”

Using the 2019 FoCoMX App

This years app has six main sections. We've already discussed the ABOUT and SPONSORS sections, we will focus on the other four app sections. Click each bar below to learn a bit more.

Music Schedule and Favorites List

The FoCoMX festival has hundreds of shows every year. Although it is great to have options, having so many shows can also make it difficult to find specific shows and remember what shows you want to go to. This years app allows you to search by band name as well as filter by day and time. Clear filters using the small red circle. When you find a show you want to go to simply click the small heart and it will be added to your favorites list. The main schedule shows all shows, the favorites list will only be the shows you have selected. You can click on the artist or venue name to get more information. To exit the artist or venue details page click the small X in the upper right corner of the page.

The content on each artist page will vary depending on what they have provided to the FoCoMX team. In most cases there will be a short bio, links to their website or facebook page, and demo clip of their music.


Map

The map section provides an interactive tool to find locations around town. Simply choose a locaiton from the dropdown and a pin corresponding to that location will be highlighted. FoCoMX is a great opportunity to check out locations you may not have visited before.

Michael Kirkpatrick

Rock-Pop

This is Michael's 10th FoCoMX. Michael Kirkpatrick is best known as the 2014 Telluride Troubadour, and ringleader of Holler!ween--a Northern Colorado music festival in its 16th year. Michael's sonic affair with music has brought his rich baritone into The Santa Fe Opera, Folk clubs, Rock'nRoll venues and Bluegrass Festivals. Performing over 250 dates a year on guitars, mandolins and voice, Michael brings it all to the theater: sweat, sensuality, tears, and sometimes blood.
